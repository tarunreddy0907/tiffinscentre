SPECIAL JINI DOSA

The South Indian Jini Dosa is a delightful twist on the traditional dosa. This fusion creation is a flavorful medley of dosa, stuffed with a mix of vegetables and spices, and then toasted to perfection. It's a crunchy, savory delight, often served with a side of tangy chutneys and a dollop of creamy butter. The Jini Dosa offers a unique culinary experience, combining the classic dosa with innovative flavors, making it a must-try for those looking to explore the diverse and delicious world of South Indian cuisine. Whether enjoyed as a snack or a meal, South Indian Jini Dosa is a favorite among food enthusiasts and a testament to the rich culinary heritage of the region.

PURI

South Indian puri, or poori, is a beloved deep-fried bread. These small, round delights are made from unleavened wheat dough, offering a crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior. Typically served for breakfast or brunch, they pair perfectly with flavorful potato masala. Their versatility makes them ideal for both everyday meals and festive occasions, reflecting South India's rich culinary heritage. Whether served with chutneys, sambar, or other regional delicacies, puris promise a delightful culinary experience.
